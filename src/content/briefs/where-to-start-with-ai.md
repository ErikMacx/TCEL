Start with a decision, not a tool. Find the two or three choices the group makes that are slow, expensive or inconsistent today, and get clear on what a better version of that decision looks like.

Only then ask whether AI helps. Sometimes it does, sometimes a simpler change does. Leading with the tool gets you pilots in search of a purpose; leading with the decision gets you advantage.
