A headcount target is the easy part and the least durable. Real Qatarisation is a capability pipeline: structured development, real responsibility, and succession planned years out.

Done well, nationals run the business because they are the best people to run it, not because a quota required a name on a chart. That is the difference between a programme that lasts and one that is quietly reversed.
